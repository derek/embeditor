Embeditor

A super easy way to embed a text editor onto any webpage!

Ok. How?

            
    <iframe src="http://embeditor.org/"></iframe>

That's it! Well, almost. There's even a simple Embeditor JavaScript API that you can include into your page as well to give you access to the underlying editor.
